The Best Matcha Latte Recipe

This creamy, delicious matcha latte will energize your mornings with its rich flavor and healthy benefits.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp high-quality matcha powder
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Whisk matcha powder with hot water until smooth.
  2. Heat almond milk until warm.
  3. Add honey or maple syrup to the milk.
  4. Pour matcha mixture into the milk and whisk until frothy.
